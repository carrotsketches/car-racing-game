Seahorse Splash 🐚

Pop bubbles, dodge jellyfish!

Glide your seahorse with your finger to pop bubbles. 🐚

How to Play

Move your finger or mouse to glide the seahorse through the ocean. Touch rising bubbles to pop them — small zippy bubbles are worth the most! Watch out for drifting jellyfish 🪼 — bumping one costs a heart. Pop as many bubbles as you can before time runs out or you lose all 3 hearts.
