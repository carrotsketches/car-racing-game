Seahorse Splash 🌊

Pop bubbles, make seahorse friends, dodge jellyfish!

Glide around — pop bubbles and make seahorse friends! 💞

How to Play

Move your finger or mouse to glide the seahorse through the ocean. Touch rising bubbles to pop them — small zippy bubbles are worth the most! Swim up to the colorful seahorses 💞 to make friends — they follow you in a happy train (+2 each). Watch out for drifting jellyfish 🪼 — they'll slow you down! Collect friends and pop as many bubbles as you can. Press 🏁 Done whenever you're ready to finish.
