How to Play

Each round shows a row of pictures with one or two ❓ blanks. Look at the repeating pattern, then tap the picture that fills each ❓ (left to right). Get the whole pattern right on the first try for +10, with one slip for +5. After two misses the answer lights up and you move on. 10 rounds per game.
