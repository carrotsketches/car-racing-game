How to Play

Each round shows a little picture puzzle: a pattern to finish, an odd one out, a counting row, a size sequence, bigger/smaller, same/different, or odd-count-out. Tap the answer tile at the bottom. Get it right on the first try for +10, second try for +5. After two misses the correct tile lights up and you move on. 10 rounds per game.
