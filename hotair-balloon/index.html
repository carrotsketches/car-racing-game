How to Play

Hold Space, tap the canvas, or hold the 🔥 button to fire the burner — your balloon rises while you hold.

Release to let it drift down naturally.

Birds 🐦 and storm clouds ⛈️ now give gentle bumps instead of taking hearts.

Collect ⭐ stars with the next correct letter shown in the HUD to spell the whole sight word.

Your score is the distance flown plus star bonuses. Speed increases over time!
