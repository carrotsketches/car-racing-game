Ready to Dig?

Rotate the arm over the dirt, press DIG to scoop. Swing to the truck and press DUMP to unload. Fill 4 trucks for a big bonus!

◀ ▶ to swing the arm · ⛏ to dig dirt · dump into the truck!

How to Play

Rotate: Tap ◀ / ▶ (or press A / D or ← / → keys) to swing the excavator arm.

Dig: Swing the bucket over the dirt pile on the left, then tap ⛏ (or press Space) to scoop a load.

Dump: Swing back right over the truck bed and tap ⛏ to dump the dirt in.

Fill the truck with 4 loads to send it off and earn a bonus. Score as many points as you can in 60 seconds!
